Corporate Overview

Marcopolo Tiles has been no.1 Zambian tile manufacturing company under wonderful group of industries, Marcopolo Tiles is known for its polished porcelains, glazed porcelain and matt porcelain tiles with exclusive designs. Wonderful group is not only into manufacturing of tiles but also pvc ceilings ,wall skimming (Interior/Exterior) and also the tile fix and the required accessories for the ceilings.
